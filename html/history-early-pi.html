Pi: Paradise for number lovers

A history of pi-ness:

 

Early Pi –

It is impossible to know when man first realised that as a circle got larger, so did its diameter and circumference and that they grew at a constant proportion to each other.

But humans must have realised something about proportion and how it affects things.

For example a rock is heavy but a rock twice its size is twice as heavy, and if 3 fields can feed a single village then 6 fields could feed 2 villages etc.

So someone before recorded time, must have seen that a circle twice the size of another has the same proportions/ratios.
