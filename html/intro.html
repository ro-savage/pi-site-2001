Introduction

Pi has fascinated man since the dawn of it’s time. A simple problem of dividing a circle’s circumference by its diameter. Yet no-one has or ever will discover the true value of Pi. Pi is such a simple concept yet there are those who have dedicated their whole lives to p and its mysteries. Sure we have calculated Pi to its 206 billionth digit, yet we have only just scratched the surface of the true mysteries of p. Why do we continue to calculate Pi, even though with 100 digits of Pi we could make a circle the size of the universe and it would only be a few meters off perfection.
